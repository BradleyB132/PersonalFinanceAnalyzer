### User Story 1
As a user,
I want to be able to upload my bank statements 
so that I can have my transactions automatically categorized and visualized on the dashboard.

### Acceptance Criteria
- **AC1:** Given that I am on the dashboard, 
  When I click on the "Upload Bank Statement" button, 
  Then I should be prompted to select a file from my computer.
- **AC2:** Given that I have selected a valid bank statement file, 
  When I submit the upload form, 
  Then my transactions should be processed and categorized correctly.
- **AC3:** Given that my transactions have been processed, 
  When I view the dashboard, 
  Then I should see visualizations of my spending trends and categorized transactions.
